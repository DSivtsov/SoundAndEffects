Information:
It's possible to play in two modes: Online and Offline. In the Offline mode results will store locally only ({TopList Local}).
In the Online mode is exist the possibility to copy results to Leader board in Internet ({TopList Global}).
On the Leader board will be stored the best player's result only.
In {Settings} you can select {playMode} and other parameters.
In {Help} section you can find information about Score counting and used interface in Game.

Note(!!!):
Before the first start of the game, a new player must be created ({New User)}
